About this timeline & sources

Primary source: Divine Ascent podcast, “Indian Church History” — Part 1, Part 2, Part 3, Part 4. Every podcast citation links to the exact chapter timestamp in the episode video (shown as minutes:seconds). Dates and names were verified against published references, cited per event, including Wikipedia: Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church, mosc.in (official church history), OrthodoxWiki timeline, and others. Where the podcast and published sources differ, a purple note flags the discrepancy. Events before the 16th century rest largely on church tradition and early textual witnesses; they are presented as the tradition holds them, with that caveat.
